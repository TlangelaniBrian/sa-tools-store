## Short Description (for marketplace listing)

Stop guessing on your next buy-to-let. This professional South African property investment calculator gives you instant clarity on bond repayments, real rental yields, monthly cash flow, and 10-year equity projections — all built around current South African rates and SARS 2024/25 transfer duty scales.

Enter your purchase price and watch it auto-calculate transfer duty, conveyancing costs, levy, rates, maintenance provisions, and vacancy losses. See your break-even rental figure, cash-on-cash return on your deposit, and exactly what the property will be worth — and how much equity you will hold — in 5 and 10 years.

Built for South African investors, agents, and buyers who need real numbers before they commit.

**What you get:** A fully unlocked Excel workbook and Google Sheets version with amortisation schedule, multi-property comparison (up to 5 deals), tax scenario modelling, and a printable one-page investment report.
