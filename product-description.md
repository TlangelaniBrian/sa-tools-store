# SA Income Tax Calculator 2025/26 — Product Description

## Short Listing Description

**Stop guessing what lands in your bank account.** The SA Income Tax Calculator gives South African employees an instant, accurate breakdown of their PAYE, UIF, and medical aid credits for the 2025/26 tax year — all in one clean, professional spreadsheet.

Built on the official SARS 2025/26 tax tables, it calculates your exact income tax across all seven brackets, applies the correct rebates for your age group, subtracts medical aid tax credits for every covered dependant, and caps your UIF at the legislated R177.12/month ceiling. Enter your salary, select your age group, and get your real monthly take-home pay in seconds.

**Included:** Google Sheets calculator with full formula workings, three-scenario comparison tool, reference tables for all tax brackets and rebates, and a printable payslip summary.

**Who it's for:** Salaried employees evaluating a new offer, HR professionals running payroll estimates, and anyone who wants to understand their deductions before payday.
