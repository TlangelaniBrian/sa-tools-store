SARS Compliance Requirements

⚠️ What SARS requires in your vehicle logbook

  • Date of each trip
  • Starting point (origin address or description)
  • Ending point (destination address or description)
  • Purpose of the trip (business or private)
  • Odometer reading at the start of the trip
  • Odometer reading at the end of the trip
  • Total kilometres travelled per trip
  • Opening odometer on 1 March and closing odometer on 28/29 February for the full tax year
  • Records must be kept for 5 years after the tax return is submitted (s73 TAA)
  • The 2025 SARS deemed cost rate is R4.84 per km for vehicles up to 3,500kg (ITR12 source code 3701/3702)
